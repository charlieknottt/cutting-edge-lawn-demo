Our Services

From concept to completion, we deliver comprehensive landscaping solutions for residential and commercial properties across Auburn, AL.

Design

Landscape Design

Our design team creates full-color drawings that bring your vision to life before any ground is broken. We provide detailed plant and hardscape recommendations tailored to your property, climate, and lifestyle.

Whether you want to transform your entire property or tackle it in stages, we offer phased planning that fits your timeline and budget, so every step moves you closer to the outdoor space you deserve.

Installation

Landscape Installation

We handle residential, commercial, and industrial landscape installations of every scale. From sod and trees to shrubs, irrigation systems, hardscapes, drainage, and outdoor lighting, our crews execute each project with precision.

As a licensed General Contractor, we bring the expertise and accountability needed to deliver lasting results on time and on budget.

Maintenance

Lawn Maintenance

Keep your property looking its best year-round with our ongoing lawn care programs. We deploy multiple crews equipped with professional-grade walker mowers and heavy equipment to maintain properties of all sizes.

Consistent, reliable service means your landscape stays healthy, clean, and sharp without you lifting a finger.

Hardscapes

Hardscapes & Water Features

Add structure and character to your outdoor space with custom patios, walkways, retaining walls, and fire pits. These elements define your landscape and create functional areas for entertaining and relaxation.

We also design and build fountains, ponds, and streams that bring the calming presence of water to your property, turning any yard into a personal retreat.

Irrigation

Irrigation & Drainage

Proper water management is the foundation of a healthy landscape. We design and install professional irrigation systems that deliver the right amount of water exactly where it is needed, reducing waste and promoting strong root growth.

Our drainage solutions protect your property from standing water, erosion, and foundation damage, keeping your landscape thriving through every season.

Lawn Care

Weed Control & Fertilization

A thick, green lawn starts with the right treatment program. Our specialized weed control and fertilization services target unwanted growth while feeding your turf the nutrients it needs to thrive.

We develop customized treatment plans based on your soil, grass type, and local conditions to deliver a healthy, weed-free lawn you can be proud of.
