Our Story

Over 20 Years of Transforming Outdoor Spaces

Founded with an Auburn University horticulture degree and a passion for the outdoors, Cutting Edge Lawn Service has grown from a one-person operation into a full-service landscape company with multiple maintenance crews, dedicated landscaping crews, irrigation specialists, and turfgrass specialists.

Our mission is to transform outdoor spaces into captivating experiences. Whether residential, commercial, or industrial, we bring the same level of care and expertise to every project. As a Licensed General Contractor, we have the credentials and capability to handle jobs of any scale.

We invest in the best equipment to deliver the best results, from Walker mowers and heavy equipment to a full fleet of trucks that keep our crews moving efficiently across Auburn and the surrounding area.
